Make Gaming Safe

I think we can all agree that gaming as a whole is a very toxic environment that some people may be hesitant to join such a non welcoming community. Our main goal is to make gaming a safe space for everyone, so we can all enjoy our favorite games together as one. We know that we cannot accomplish this on our own, but with your help anything is possible.

If you share this website with your friends, family, or peers it could help change the course of gaming forever. By spreading awareness on this topic we bring new perspectives into the equation. If we see how others feel we can easily find a solution to the monstrosity that is the gaming culture.

We are pleased to announce that we have many opportunities coming your way in the future that sadly cannot be disclosed yet. However if you sign the Volunteer form you will be the first to know how we, as a whole, will change gaming forever.

We thank you for joining us on our journey to make gaming a safe space for everyone.

About Us

We are a non profitable organization that tries to bring positivity to gaming. Today marks the journey to do so, and would be incredibly grateful if you decided to join us on this impactful journey.

We have mods available 24/7 monitoring chats and voice rooms so that this website is a safe place for gamers to come and enjoy gaming in a safe environment.

We hire only the best fits so that we can ensure that everything is executed perfectly. As a whole, our staff puts full effort into this cause and they are highly motivated to make you feel welcomed.
